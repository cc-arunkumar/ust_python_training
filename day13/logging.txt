DEBUG:
Level 10 to trace variable values or program flow
eg: connecting to db

INFO:
level 20 to show progress or actions performed
ex: logger.INFO("user login")

WARNING:
level 30 Something unexpected happened (might become a problem but prgram executes)
ex: logger.WARNING("low disc space")

ERROR:
level 40 serious issue occured a part of program failed , program continue running
ex: logger.ERROR("failed to read the file")

CRITICAL:
level 50 program crashed, immediate action required since system is unusable
logger.CRITICAL("server down")
