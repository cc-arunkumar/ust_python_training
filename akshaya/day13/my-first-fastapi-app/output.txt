
FastAPI Utility Endpoints - Example Output


1.  Home

Request:
GET /

Response:
{"message": "Hyyyyyyy"}

2.  Greet

Request:
GET /greet/Asha

Response:
{"message": "Asha"}

3.  Addition

Request:
GET /add?a=2&b=3&c=4

Response:
{"sum=": 9}

4.  Square (with path parameter)

Request:
GET /square/5

Response:
{"sq": 25}

5.  Square (with default value)

Request:

{"sq": 64}

Request:
